Get to know me

Hello you beautiful people, I am Clarenz Tandoc (The one on the right) but most people know me by "CJ". I am an aspiring Software Engineer, Software Developer, and Web Developer from San Jose, California. Ever since I was a child, I have always been curious about how machines and computers work. It thrills me when I learn something new about technology. I was originally from the outskirts of the Philippines, where we harvest our own food and play with farm animals. I was lucky enough to migrate in the United States of America during my sophomore year of High School and continue my education. Although learning another language beyond different than the one I grew up with, I was able to learn and speak english fluently. I quickly applied the same tactics I have used in learning english into learning how to code. The languages I currently used within my projects are JavaScript, CSS, HTML5, and Python. I also have experience with C++ and Arduino Programming. If you are interested in my projects and my work, click this link!
